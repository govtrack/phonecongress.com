Privacy Policy

PhoneCongress.com collects typical non-personal information needed to maintain a healthy website, such as your IP address, web browser, and referral URL. We also ask for your home address or location to determine your congressional district. We do not store your home address or location on our servers, but it is stored in your browser.

This site collects the same information whether or not your browser sends a Do Not Track header. When you visit this website, other parties whose resources we have embeded may be collecting information about you as well. We avoid embedding resources that have egregious tracking policies.
